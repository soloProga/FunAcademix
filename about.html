About Funacademix

Who We Are ?

Funacademix is an online tutoring website designed to help solve the issue of children lagging or struggling or needing extra tutoring with online schooling.

Funacademix has three points of differentiation: The class sizes are small, max 15 students per class, which allows students to receive personal attention if necessary.

Funacademix is specialised in only two subjects are taught Mathematics, and English, which students or parents can choose from when booking appointments, and it is game-oriented which makes it fun and interactive for students, by using Kahoot to create mini games, Quizalize to post homework assignments or quizzes. Funacademix's target market is parents of primary school students in class 3 and class 4.
